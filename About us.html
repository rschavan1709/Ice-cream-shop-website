Cool Cafe

Cool Cafe is a very popular kulfies shop. located in Thane city, Cool Cafe is synonymous to impeccable quality,taste and hygiene. With over 2 decades of expertise and experience, Cool Cafe offers a wide varieties of kulfies that transcend the typical to traditional to innovative.

The brainchild of Mrs. Snehal More, the Kulfies Shop is a benchmark for personal attention and customer satisfaction. The crowds that throng the shop on any given day and the beeline queue seen during summer days speaks volumes about the relationships Cool Cafe has built over the years.

Be it a festival, family celebration or corporate event, Cool Cafe is the first choice of the quality conscious.
